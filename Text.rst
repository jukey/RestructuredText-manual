====================================================
Syntax
====================================================

Text
----
    
       An extremely awesome sentence. Another mega-awesome sentence.
       Lines that are together form a paragraph.
    
       Lines that are apart form separate paragraphs.

Text Highlightning
~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~~

  * *emphasized text* : ``*emphasized text*``
  * **strong text**   : ``**strong text**``
  * ``literal text``  :: 

     ``literal text``
